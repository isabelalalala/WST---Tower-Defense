Gameplay Guide

How to Play

01
🦠

Know Your Enemy

Waves of pathogens — bacteria and viruses — are invading the body. They travel along a fixed path trying to reach the bloodstream.

02
🛡️

Deploy Defenses

Place immune cells along the path to stop the invaders. Each cell type has unique abilities — use them wisely to hold the line.

03
⚡

Earn & Upgrade

Defeating pathogens earns you resources. Spend them to place more immune cells or upgrade existing ones to handle tougher waves.

04
❤️

Protect the Body

You have limited health points. If too many pathogens break through, the body gets sick. Survive all waves to win!
